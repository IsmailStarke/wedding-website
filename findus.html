Where To Find Us?

 Please Note:

There are multiple Qurtuba establishments in the Linbro Park vicinity. For your convenience, if you're approaching via 2nd Avenue from 3rd Road, take a left onto 2nd Road. After approximately 50 meters, the Convention Centre will be on your left.

If you're traveling straight along 2nd Road from Malboro Drive, you'll pass 1st Avenue and then 2nd Avenue. About 50 meters ahead, the Convention Centre will be on your left.

Below the map we have provided an image for reference. You may also click on the directions button in the top left of the map to use your GPS.
